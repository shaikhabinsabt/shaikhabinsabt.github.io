Industrial Aesthetics

Reimagining Overlooked Industrial & Disaster Sites

About

I explore the hidden beauty in industrial and disaster-prone sites such as oil refineries, emphasizing contradictions and overlooked aesthetics. My process integrates CNC milling, resin casting, and laser cutting to create tactile architectural models and artifacts that challenge traditional perceptions.

Selected Projects

Abstract Refinery Model: A detailed CNC-milled model exploring the formal beauty of an oil refinery’s structural complexity.

Resin Casting Artifact: Using resin to capture textures and forms inspired by disaster-affected materials, blending organic and industrial aesthetics.

Laser-Cut Architectural Study: Laser-cut layered explorations highlighting fragmentation and reconstruction in industrial landscapes.
